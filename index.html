Term Examination For Classes 1 to 12 Begins On Sep 29 ,2021

Principal's Message

On behalf of the entire K.E. School faculty and staff, I would like to welcome all my adorable children and your families to the new academic session of 2021-22. We, at K. E. always envisage an all around and balanced education based on sound intellectual, physical, moral and social formation.

The aim is to make you erudite, patriotic, law abiding and ceaselessly seeking excellence, with a deep concern for all especially the weak and the poor sections of the society. And to my mind, education as an idea, is not just about bricks ,mortar and concrete , but about building character , enriching minds and about varied experiences that last a lifetime.

COVID-19 Pandemic has surely altered our lives and disrupted normal life across the world. We understand the challenges faced by you, your parents and the teachers to cater to the academic needs during closure of school and adapting to virtual learning. But I am proud at the same time as you continued to demonstrate diligence, motivation and determination to work to the best of your abilities to learn new instructions.

K.E. assures that, the new academic year 2021-22 is really going to make your each second worthwhile as the school has taken initiative in many well planned activities and programmes for all to ponder over, act upon and meet with. I would like to see my children soaring new heights and tasting success in all their endeavours under the guidance and care of our dedicated and hard working teachers. Let this Almanac be a step further mentioning all the great plans we put forward for our children to achieve the goals expected.
